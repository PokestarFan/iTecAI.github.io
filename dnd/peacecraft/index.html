History of Aladen

Welcome to Aladen. On Aladen, four primary sentient races rule (although others exist and have distinct cultures of their own): the Fey, dwarves, humans, and halflings. The elves and humans reside primarily in the Kingdom of Ardour and its surrounding islands, while the halflings and dwarves live primarily on the continent of Morkaria, although dwarves tend to roam across both lands to mine valuable resources. The current year is 1334, year 0 marking the conquest of the mountain giants of Ardour by the elven king Grennon. In the year 988, the Novan Land Bridge rose from the seas to the East of Ardour. Two years later, in 990, an envoy of elves from Elkannas discovered that there were lands even further East, populated by dwarves and halflings. Despite resistance from the residents, the nearest island to Ardour was colonized, and a new major city was constructed: New Algorran. Some humans, amazed by the power of the sea flowing between the Nordsi and the Sudski, moved to a small island east of New Algorran. These humans called themselves the Children of Neptos, the name of a minor water demon that had supposedly revealed itself to them. They enslave halflings for menial labor and sacrifices for Neptos, and are currently ruled by the High priestess Amalis. Although very little is known by Ardour about the continent of Morkaria, there are tales that say an evil power holds sway over some areas, and that there may even be entrances to the fabled Underdark.
